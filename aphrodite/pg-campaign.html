Why study a masters?

Enhanced earning potential

Students with a postgraduate degree can expect to earn £5,500 more a year

This is compared to those with just a bachelor's – that's around £200,000 in a working lifetime (source: Sutton Trust). A masters won't just help you to get a job, it will also help you to earn more money.

Career-changing possibilities

In 2021, 77.4% of working-age postgraduates are in high-skilled employment

Compared with 65.2% of working-age graduates (source: gov.uk). A masters is an opportunity to build on your knowledge and become an expert in your discipline; you're also more likely to be in high-skilled employment.

Career progression

Use a postgraduate degree to change career

Not every course requires you to have studied the subject before. We offer career-changing courses in law, teaching, physiotherapy, podiatry, occupational therapy, social work and journalism.
