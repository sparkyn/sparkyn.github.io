Online open day – 21 October 2020

Today's open day will run from 12.30 to 4.30pm.

Welcome to our online open day. We're so pleased to see you. On this page you'll find links to live and on-demand activities about your subject and university life at Brighton. And we'll be on hand with information, top tips and to answer all of your questions. 

We suggest you start with our brilliant VC, Professor Debra Humphris as she officially welcomes you to the University of Brighton. And then join your subject Q+A between 1 and 2.30pm.

Take a look at our top tips to make sure that your day goes smoothly.

Your open day checklist

These key activities will help you get the most out of your open day:

  • Watch our Vice-Chancellor's welcome and your subject video
  • Register for Unibuddy and log in ready for our live chat sessions
  • Subject talks will be held on Microsoft Teams. You may need to download the Microsoft Teams app to access them, particularly if you are joining us via your smartphone.
  • Watch your live subject talk(s)
  • Watch our Unibuddy livestreams on accommodation, finance and student life
  • Come to our virtual student life and advice live chat to ask anything about life at uni
  • Watch our on-demand videos and have a look at our virtual campus and facilities tours

Our live talks today are arranged by subject area below. If you aren't sure which subject area contains your course, choose your subject from the drop-down below to find the links.
